I believe that life is a racetrack where you strain to outrun yourself. You shouldn’t look up to or down at anyone else as a measure of your success. I am a girl in a society of suppressed females who has been blessed with opportunity .I am only what my triumphs and failures have led me to be. I initially wanted to do Biomedical Engineering but this degree wasn’t recognized in Pakistan. Life closed and opened new doors for me and Alhamdulillah now I am at the School of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (SEECS), NUST, in my second year of study with a major in Software engineering. I am a highly motivated second year student looking for real corporate experience to expand the horizon of my capabilities. Along with studies, I do firmly believe that extra curricula’s are of immense importance. Joining different societies is one way to allow for this. I hold an Executive post in the SEECS Literary Society and also the post of Assistant Director of Media and Creativity at the NUST Community Service “Edvolution” Chapter
